Body Mass Index

BMI

The BMI is a convenient rule of thumb used to broadly categorize a person as underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese based on tissue mass (muscle, fat, and bone) and height.

BMI Calculator

Normal BMI lies between 18.5 to 25.

BMI Guide

  • If Your BMI is less than 18.5,you fall within the underweight range.
  • If Your BMI is in between 18.5 to 25,you fall within the normal.
  • If Your BMI lies between 25 to 30 ,you fall within the overweight range.
  • If Your BMI is 30 or higher,it falls within the obesity range.
